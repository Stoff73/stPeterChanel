Our Mission

"Inspire, Challenge & Empower so through Christ we make a difference"

At St Peter Chanel, we believe we are all children of God and together we create a loving Christian community underpinned by common Gospel values.

Our Vision

Through the love of Christ we can Inspire | Challenge | Empower

Our Aims

Inspire

To educate children to reach their full potential through a memorable and exciting curriculum that challenges and inspires.

Challenge

To maintain high expectations for all in their learning, attitudes and pursuit of excellence.

Spiritual Growth

To nurture spiritual and moral development so that we all act out the Christ in ourselves and see the Christ in others, and develop a deep and confident relationship with God.

Empower

To empower children to be independent learners and thinkers who take responsibility for their own learning.

Our Values

Our Catholic faith is at the heart of everything we do. We promote Gospel values including:

  • Love - We show love to God, ourselves, and each other
  • Respect - We respect ourselves, others, and our environment
  • Compassion - We show kindness and understanding to all
  • Forgiveness - We forgive others as God forgives us
  • Service - We serve others in our community and beyond
  • Truth - We are honest and truthful in all we do
  • Justice - We stand up for what is right and fair

Our Community

All children, from all faiths and no faiths, are welcome at St Peter Chanel. We work closely with our parish, St Lawrence's in Sidcup, and are a proud member of St Oscar Romero Catholic Academy Trust.
