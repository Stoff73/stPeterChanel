Relationships and Behaviour

Home/About Us/Behaviour

At St Peter Chanel, we believe that positive relationships are at the heart of a happy, successful school community.

Our Approach

Our approach to behaviour is based on our Catholic values of love, respect, forgiveness and reconciliation. We believe that every child is made in the image of God and deserves to be treated with dignity and respect.

School Rules

Our simple school rules help everyone to be safe, happy and successful:

  • Be Ready - Come to school prepared to learn
  • Be Respectful - Treat others as you would like to be treated
  • Be Safe - Keep yourself and others safe

Positive Recognition

We believe in celebrating success and recognising positive behaviour. This includes:

  • Verbal praise and encouragement
  • House points
  • Certificates and awards
  • Recognition in assembly
  • Communication with parents about positive achievements

Restorative Approach

When things go wrong, we use a restorative approach to help children understand the impact of their actions and make amends. This helps children learn from mistakes and develop empathy.

Anti-Bullying

We take bullying very seriously and have robust procedures in place to prevent and address any incidents. We teach children about different types of bullying and how to seek help.
