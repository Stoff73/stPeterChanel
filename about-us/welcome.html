Welcome From The Headteacher

Home/ About Us/ Headteacher's Welcome

Mr Davies

Headteacher

It is my pleasure to welcome you to St Peter Chanel Catholic Primary School and Preschool.

It is a pleasure to lead such a welcoming and caring community and we all look forward to a rewarding and successful year.

St Peter Chanel is a thriving one-form entry school with an on-site Pre-School. Our extensive grounds allow us to provide stimulating opportunities for outdoor learning. We work closely with our parish, St Lawrence's in Sidcup, and benefit from strong links that provide rich opportunities for both the school and church, giving us all a strong sense of community.

We are a Catholic School where we believe we are all children of God and together we create a loving Christian community underpinned by common Gospel values. All children, from all faiths and no faiths, are welcome.

"Through the love of Christ we can Inspire | Challenge | Empower"

Our Vision

At St Peter Chanel, we aim to:

  • Educate children to reach their full potential through a memorable and exciting curriculum that challenges and inspires
  • Maintain high expectations for all in their learning, attitudes and pursuit of excellence
  • Nurture spiritual and moral development so that we all act out the Christ in ourselves and see the Christ in others, and develop a deep and confident relationship with God
  • Empower children to be independent learners and thinkers who take responsibility for their own learning

Visit Us

If you are interested in joining our school community, I warmly invite you to visit us. We hold regular open mornings for prospective parents, or you can arrange a personal tour by contacting the school office.

I look forward to welcoming you to St Peter Chanel.

Mr Davies
Headteacher
