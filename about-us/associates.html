Our Associates

Home/About Us/Associates

St Peter Chanel works with a number of partner organisations to enhance the education and wellbeing of our children.

St Oscar Romero Catholic Academy Trust

We are a proud member of the St Oscar Romero Catholic Academy Trust, which brings together Catholic schools committed to providing excellent education rooted in Gospel values.

Visit Trust Website

Corpus Christi Partnership

We are part of the Corpus Christi Partnership, a family of Catholic schools in our local area. This partnership enables collaboration, sharing of best practice, and smooth transitions for our pupils.

Partner Secondary Schools

  • St Catherine's Catholic School
  • St Columba's Catholic Boys' School

Our Parish

St Lawrence's Church, Sidcup

We have strong links with our parish and work closely together on sacramental preparation, community events, and supporting families.

Diocese of Southwark

As a Catholic school, we work within the Diocese of Southwark, which provides support, guidance and training for Catholic education across South London and beyond.

Local Authority

We work with the London Borough of Bexley on various aspects of education provision, including admissions, SEND support, and safeguarding.

Other Partners

  • Sports partnership providers
  • Music services
  • Educational psychologists and support services
  • Local community organisations
