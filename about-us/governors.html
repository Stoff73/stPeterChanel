Home/ About Us/ Governors

Our Local Governing Body works in partnership with the school leadership and St Oscar Romero Catholic Academy Trust to ensure the best outcomes for all children.

Governance Structure

As an academy within the St Oscar Romero Catholic Academy Trust, our governance structure includes:

  • Trust Board - Overall accountability for all schools in the Trust
  • Local Governing Body - Focused oversight of our individual school
  • School Leadership Team - Day-to-day management and operations

Local Governing Body

Our Local Governing Body consists of:

  • Foundation Governors (appointed by the Diocese)
  • Parent Governors (elected by parents)
  • Staff Governor
  • Headteacher

Role of Governors

Our governors are responsible for:

  • Ensuring clarity of vision, ethos and strategic direction
  • Holding the Headteacher to account for the educational performance of the school
  • Overseeing the financial performance of the school
  • Ensuring the school fulfils its statutory duties
  • Supporting the Catholic life and mission of the school

Meetings

The Local Governing Body meets at least once per term. Minutes of meetings are available from the school office.

Contact the Governors

If you wish to contact the Chair of Governors, please write to them via the school office or email admin@st-peterchanel.bexley.sch.uk marking your correspondence for the attention of the Chair of Governors.

Become a Governor

We are always looking for committed individuals to join our governing body. If you are interested in becoming a governor, please contact the school office for more information.
