Welcome to St Peter Chanel

St Peter Chanel is a thriving one-form entry Catholic primary school with an on-site Pre-School, located in the London Borough of Bexley.

We are a Catholic School where we believe we are all children of God and together we create a loving Christian community underpinned by common Gospel values. All children, from all faiths and no faiths, are welcome.

Our extensive grounds allow us to provide stimulating opportunities for outdoor learning. We work closely with our parish, St Lawrence's in Sidcup, and benefit from strong links that provide rich opportunities for both the school and church, giving us all a strong sense of community.

We are a proud member of St Oscar Romero Catholic Academy Trust and part of the Corpus Christi Partnership, a collaborative network of Catholic schools in the area.

Our Mission

"Inspire, Challenge & Empower so through Christ we make a difference"

Our Aims

  • To educate children to reach their full potential through a memorable and exciting curriculum that challenges and inspires
  • To maintain high expectations for all in their learning, attitudes and pursuit of excellence
  • To nurture spiritual and moral development so that we all act out the Christ in ourselves and see the Christ in others
  • To empower children to be independent learners and thinkers who take responsibility for their own learning
