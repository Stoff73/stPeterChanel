Music

Home/Curriculum/Music

Music is an important part of school life at St Peter Chanel. We believe every child should have the opportunity to enjoy and create music.

Our Music Vision

Music enriches children's lives and supports their development. Our music curriculum:

  • Develops a love of music and appreciation of different genres
  • Builds skills in singing, playing instruments and composing
  • Teaches musical knowledge and vocabulary
  • Provides performance opportunities
  • Supports worship and liturgy

What We Teach

Singing

Developing vocal skills and confidence

Playing

Tuned and untuned percussion

Composing

Creating original music

Listening

Appreciating different music

Musical Elements

Children learn about the key elements of music:

  • Pitch: High and low sounds
  • Rhythm: Patterns and beats
  • Tempo: Speed of music
  • Dynamics: Loud and quiet
  • Timbre: Different sounds and instruments
  • Texture: Layers of sound
  • Structure: How music is organised

Performances

Children have opportunities to perform throughout the year:

  • Singing in assemblies and Masses
  • Christmas and Easter concerts
  • Class assemblies
  • School choir performances

Instrumental Tuition

We offer opportunities for children to learn instruments through peripatetic teachers. Instruments currently available include:

  • Recorder
  • Keyboard/Piano
  • Guitar
  • Violin

Please contact the school office for more information about instrumental lessons.

School Choir

Our school choir is open to children in KS2. See our Choir page for more information.
