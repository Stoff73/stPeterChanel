Curriculum Overview

Home/Curriculum/Overview

Our curriculum is designed to inspire, challenge and nurture every child, helping them to flourish academically, spiritually and personally.

Our Curriculum Vision

At St Peter Chanel, we believe that every child is made in the image of God and deserves a rich, broad and balanced curriculum that enables them to develop their God-given talents.

Our curriculum is designed to:

  • Develop the whole child - spiritually, morally, intellectually, physically and emotionally
  • Build strong foundations in English, Mathematics and Science
  • Provide rich learning experiences across all subjects
  • Prepare children for the next stage of their education and for life
  • Celebrate our Catholic identity and Gospel values

Curriculum Structure

We follow the National Curriculum, enhanced by our Catholic ethos and tailored to meet the needs of our children. Our curriculum is organised into:

Core Subjects

  • English
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Religious Education

Foundation Subjects

  • History
  • Geography
  • Art & DT
  • Music
  • PE
  • Computing & MFL
  • PSHE & RSE

Teaching Approaches

We use a variety of teaching approaches to engage and inspire children:

  • Quality First Teaching: High-quality teaching for all children in every lesson
  • Mastery Approach: Particularly in Mathematics, ensuring deep understanding
  • Cross-curricular Links: Connecting learning across subjects
  • Enrichment: Trips, visitors, and special events to bring learning to life
  • Outdoor Learning: Using our outdoor spaces for practical learning

Assessment

We use a range of assessment methods to track progress and identify next steps:

  • Ongoing formative assessment in lessons
  • Termly assessments in core subjects
  • Statutory assessments (Phonics Screening, KS1 SATs, Multiplication Check, KS2 SATs)
  • Regular pupil progress meetings
  • Annual reports to parents
