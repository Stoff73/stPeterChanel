Geography

Home/Curriculum/Geography

Geography helps children understand the world around them - both the physical environment and human communities. We develop curious geographers.

Our Geography Vision

We want children to develop a fascination with the world and an appreciation of God's creation. Our geography teaching:

  • Develops knowledge of places near and far
  • Builds understanding of physical and human geography
  • Teaches geographical skills including fieldwork and map skills
  • Encourages care for the environment
  • Develops global awareness and understanding of other cultures

Key Areas

Locational Knowledge

Continents, countries, cities, seas, rivers, mountains

Place Knowledge

Comparing our local area with contrasting regions

Physical Geography

Climate, weather, rivers, mountains, volcanoes, earthquakes

Human Geography

Settlements, land use, trade, resources, sustainability

Geographical Skills

  • Using maps, atlases and globes
  • Using compass directions and grid references
  • Fieldwork and observations
  • Recording and presenting data
  • Using digital mapping

Fieldwork

We believe learning geography should include first-hand experience. Children undertake fieldwork in:

  • Our school grounds
  • The local area
  • Contrasting localities through trips
