Religious Education

Home/Curriculum/Religious Education

Religious Education is at the heart of our curriculum. It is not just a subject but the foundation of everything we do.

Our RE Vision

As a Catholic school, Religious Education has a unique place in our curriculum. We follow the Religious Education Curriculum Directory for Catholic Schools and aim to:

  • Develop knowledge and understanding of the Catholic faith
  • Nurture spiritual growth and a personal relationship with God
  • Understand the Bible and its relevance to our lives
  • Learn about other world religions and promote respect for all faiths
  • Apply Gospel values to everyday life

Our Curriculum

We follow the 'Come and See' programme, which is the primary resource for RE in Catholic schools. Each term explores different themes:

Autumn

  • Family / Domestic Church
  • Belonging / Baptism
  • Advent and Christmas

Spring

  • Local Church / Community
  • Eucharist / Relating
  • Lent and Easter

Summer

  • Pentecost / Serving
  • Reconciliation / Inter-relating
  • Universal Church

Other Faiths

We also teach children about other major world religions, helping them to understand and respect the beliefs of others. We study:

  • Judaism
  • Islam
  • Hinduism
  • Sikhism
  • Buddhism

Prayer and Worship

Prayer is woven throughout our school day:

  • Daily prayers in class
  • Weekly whole school assemblies
  • Class liturgies and Masses
  • Celebrations of the liturgical year
  • Prayer corners in every classroom

Sacramental Preparation

We work with our parish to prepare children for the Sacraments of Reconciliation and First Holy Communion in Year 3. This is done in partnership with parents and the parish community.
