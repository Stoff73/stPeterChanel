Art & Design Technology

Home/Curriculum/Art & DT

Art and Design Technology develop creativity, critical thinking and practical skills, enabling children to express themselves and solve problems.

Art

Our art curriculum develops children's creativity and ability to express ideas. Children learn:

  • Drawing: Using different media and techniques
  • Painting: Colour mixing, brushwork, styles
  • Sculpture: Working with clay and 3D materials
  • Printing: Relief, mono and screen printing
  • Textiles: Weaving, sewing, fabric art
  • Collage: Mixed media work

Artists We Study

Children study the work of famous artists including:

Van Gogh
Monet
Kandinsky
Hockney

Design Technology

DT develops practical skills and problem-solving. Children learn to design, make and evaluate products:

  • Mechanisms: Levers, sliders, wheels and axles
  • Structures: Building strong, stable structures
  • Textiles: Designing and making products
  • Food: Preparing healthy food, following recipes
  • Electrical systems: Simple circuits and programming

Cross-Curricular Links

Art and DT are often linked to other subjects:

  • History: Art from different periods
  • Geography: Art from different cultures
  • Science: Materials and properties
  • RE: Religious art and symbolism
