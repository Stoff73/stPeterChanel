School Choir

Home/Children/Choir

Our school choir is a vibrant group of young singers who share their musical talents at school events and in the wider community.

About Our Choir

The St Peter Chanel Choir is open to pupils in Key Stage 2 (Years 3-6) who love to sing. We meet weekly to rehearse a variety of songs, from traditional hymns to modern pop songs and everything in between.

Being part of the choir helps children develop their singing skills, confidence, teamwork and a love of music. No auditions required - just enthusiasm and commitment!

When We Meet

Rehearsals

Every Monday lunchtime

12:30pm - 1:00pm in the School Hall

Performances

Our choir performs at many events throughout the year:

School Masses

Leading the singing at our school Masses and liturgies

Christmas Concert

Our annual Christmas concert for parents and families

Care Home Visits

Bringing joy to local care home residents

Community Events

Performing at local events and celebrations

What We Sing

Our repertoire includes:

  • Traditional and contemporary hymns
  • Songs from musicals
  • Pop songs (suitable versions!)
  • Seasonal songs for Christmas, Easter and other celebrations
  • Songs in different languages

Join the Choir

If you're in Years 3-6 and would like to join the choir:

  1. Speak to your class teacher or the choir leader
  2. Come along to a rehearsal on Monday lunchtime
  3. Commit to attending rehearsals regularly
  4. Be ready to perform at school events!

Good to Know

  • No audition required - everyone is welcome
  • You will need to be available for extra rehearsals before performances
  • Performance events may be outside of school hours
  • Parents will be notified in advance of performance dates
