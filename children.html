School Council

Our School Council gives children a voice in the running of our school. Elected representatives from each class meet regularly to discuss ideas and improvements, developing leadership and democratic skills.

Choir

Our school choir is open to children in Key Stage 2 and performs at school events, local community gatherings, and special occasions throughout the year.

Outdoor Learning

Our extensive grounds provide wonderful opportunities for outdoor learning across all curriculum areas. Children benefit from:

  • Outdoor classroom spaces
  • Nature exploration areas
  • Gardening activities
  • Environmental projects
  • Forest School sessions

Extra-Curricular Activities

We offer a range of clubs and activities before and after school, including:

  • Sports clubs (football, netball, athletics)
  • Art club
  • Music lessons
  • Drama club
  • Coding club

Details of current clubs are shared termly via newsletters.
