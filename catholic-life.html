Catholic Life at SPC

Home/ Catholic Life

At St Peter Chanel, we believe we are all children of God and together we create a loving Christian community underpinned by common Gospel values.

"Through the love of Christ we can Inspire | Challenge | Empower"

Our Faith

Our Catholic faith is at the heart of everything we do at St Peter Chanel. We nurture spiritual and moral development so that we all act out the Christ in ourselves and see the Christ in others, and develop a deep and confident relationship with God.

All children, from all faiths and no faiths, are welcome. We celebrate diversity while sharing the values that unite us as a community.

Our Values

Love

We show love to God, ourselves, and each other

Faith

We trust in God and live out our Catholic faith

Service

We serve others in our community and beyond

Peace

We promote peace and reconciliation

Our Parish

We work closely with our parish, St Lawrence's Church, Sidcup, and benefit from strong links that provide rich opportunities for both the school and church, giving us all a strong sense of community.

Our parish priest regularly visits the school and leads special Masses and liturgies throughout the year.

Worship and Prayer

Prayer is an integral part of daily life at St Peter Chanel. We begin and end each day with prayer, and gather together for weekly assemblies and regular Masses.

Key Celebrations

  • Weekly whole-school assemblies
  • Class Masses and liturgies
  • Feast day celebrations
  • Advent and Lenten services
  • First Holy Communion preparation
  • Reconciliation services
  • Harvest, Christmas and Easter celebrations
