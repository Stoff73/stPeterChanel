Our Curriculum

Home/ Curriculum

At St Peter Chanel, we provide a memorable and exciting curriculum that challenges and inspires all our children to reach their full potential.

Our Curriculum Intent

Our curriculum is designed to:

  • Educate children to reach their full potential through a memorable and exciting curriculum that challenges and inspires
  • Maintain high expectations for all in their learning, attitudes and pursuit of excellence
  • Nurture spiritual and moral development
  • Empower children to be independent learners and thinkers who take responsibility for their own learning

Our extensive grounds allow us to provide many opportunities for outdoor learning, making our curriculum truly engaging and memorable.

Curriculum Overview

We follow the National Curriculum for England, enhanced by our Catholic ethos and values. Religious Education is at the heart of our curriculum.
