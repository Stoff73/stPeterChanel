Worried About a Child?

If you have concerns about a child's safety or welfare, please contact us immediately.

School: 020 8300 7280

Out of hours: Contact Bexley Social Services or the Police

At St Peter Chanel, safeguarding children is everyone's responsibility. We are committed to ensuring the welfare and safety of all children in our care.

Our Commitment

We believe that all children have the right to be safe, happy and healthy. We work together with families to ensure that children are protected from harm.

Our school follows the statutory guidance 'Keeping Children Safe in Education' and we have robust policies and procedures in place to safeguard children.

How to Report a Concern

If you have any concerns about a child's welfare, please speak to a member of our Safeguarding Team. You can:

  • Speak to any member of staff who will pass on your concerns
  • Ask to speak directly to the Designated Safeguarding Lead
  • Call the school office: 020 8300 7280
  • Email the school (mark your email as confidential)
