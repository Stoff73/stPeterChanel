Club Schedule

Clubs run during lunchtimes and after school. A new club timetable is published at the start of each term.

Day Lunchtime After School (3:15-4:15pm)
Monday Choir (KS2) Football (Y3-6)
Tuesday Chess Club Art Club (Y1-6)
Wednesday Recorder (Y3-4) Multi-Sports (Y1-2)
Thursday Reading Club Netball (Y5-6)

*This is an example timetable. Current clubs will be communicated via ParentMail.

How to Sign Up

Club sign-up information is sent via ParentMail at the start of each term. Some clubs are free while others are run by external providers and have a fee.

  • Free clubs: Run by school staff - sign up through ParentMail
  • Paid clubs: Run by external providers - sign up and pay through ParentMail

Please Note

  • Places are allocated on a first-come, first-served basis
  • Children must be collected promptly at the end of the club
  • If your child cannot attend, please let us know
  • Clubs may be cancelled due to staff training or adverse weather
