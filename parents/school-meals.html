School Meals

Home/Parents/School Meals

We provide healthy, nutritious school meals prepared fresh on site each day.

Universal Infant Free School Meals

All children in Reception, Year 1 and Year 2 are entitled to a free school meal. This is provided by the Government's Universal Infant Free School Meals (UIFSM) scheme.

School Meal Options

Our school meals are provided by our catering team and offer:

  • A choice of hot main meal including meat, fish and vegetarian options
  • A jacket potato option with various fillings
  • Fresh vegetables and salad daily
  • A choice of dessert or fresh fruit
  • Water or milk to drink

Meal Costs

For children in Years 3-6 who are not eligible for Free School Meals:

  • Daily cost: £2.50
  • Weekly cost: £12.50

Free School Meals

You may be eligible for Free School Meals if you receive certain benefits. We encourage all eligible families to register, even for Reception to Year 2 children, as this provides additional funding (Pupil Premium) to support your child.

Check Eligibility & Apply

Allergies & Dietary Requirements

Please inform the school office of any allergies or dietary requirements. Our catering team can accommodate most dietary needs including halal, vegetarian and allergen-free options.

Packed Lunches

If your child brings a packed lunch, please ensure it is healthy and balanced. We are a nut-free school, so please do not include any items containing nuts.
