Secondary Transition

Home/Parents/Secondary Transition

Moving from primary to secondary school is an exciting milestone. We work closely with Year 6 pupils and their families to ensure a smooth transition.

Preparing for Secondary School

Throughout Year 6, we help children prepare for the next stage of their education through:

  • Transition lessons and activities
  • Information sessions for parents
  • Links with local secondary schools
  • Support with the application process
  • Discussions about managing change

How to Apply

Secondary school applications are made through the London Borough of Bexley (or your home borough if you live outside Bexley).

  1. Research schools and attend open evenings
  2. Complete the Common Application Form online
  3. List up to 6 schools in order of preference
  4. Submit by the October deadline
  5. For Catholic schools, also complete the Supplementary Information Form (SIF) and return it to the school
Apply on Bexley Council Website

Supplementary Information Form (SIF)

Catholic secondary schools require a SIF in addition to the main application. This confirms your child's Catholic faith and requires a priest's signature. Please allow time for this to be completed.

Transition Support

We understand that moving schools can feel daunting. We offer additional support for:

  • Children with SEND - we liaise closely with secondary SENCOs
  • Anxious children - extra transition visits can be arranged
  • Families needing help with applications

Year 6 SATs

In May, Year 6 pupils take national tests (SATs) in Reading, Grammar, Punctuation & Spelling, and Mathematics. We hold an information evening for parents earlier in the year and provide additional support for pupils.
