Online Payments

Home/Parents/Online Payments

We use ParentMail for all online payments. This secure system allows you to pay for school meals, trips, clubs and more.

ParentMail Payments

Login to your ParentMail account to view payment items and make secure payments.

Login to ParentMail

What Can I Pay For?

  • School Meals: Pay for school dinners (Years 3-6)
  • School Trips: Pay for educational visits and residentials
  • Breakfast & After School Club: Pay for wraparound care
  • Extra-Curricular Clubs: Pay for paid clubs and activities
  • Uniform: Order and pay for uniform items
  • Donations: Make voluntary contributions

Setting Up ParentMail

When your child joins the school, you will receive an email invitation to register for ParentMail. To complete registration:

  1. Look for an email from ParentMail with the subject "Welcome to ParentMail"
  2. Click the link in the email to create your account
  3. Download the ParentMail app to your phone for easy access
  4. Enable notifications to receive important messages

Need Help?

If you are having difficulty accessing ParentMail or making payments:

  • Check your spam/junk folder for registration emails
  • Ensure you are using the email address registered with the school
  • Contact the school office for assistance

Payment Difficulties

If you are experiencing financial difficulties and are struggling to pay for school-related items, please speak to us in confidence. We may be able to help.

Cash Payments

We encourage all payments to be made online through ParentMail. However, if you are unable to pay online, please speak to the school office who can assist you.
