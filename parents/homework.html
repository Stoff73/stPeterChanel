Homework

Home/Parents/Homework

Homework is an important part of your child's learning. It helps to reinforce what has been taught in class and develops good study habits.

Our Homework Expectations

EYFS & Key Stage 1

  • Daily reading (10-15 minutes)
  • Phonics/spelling practice
  • Number bonds and counting

Lower Key Stage 2

  • Daily reading (15-20 minutes)
  • Weekly spelling practice
  • Times tables practice
  • Occasional topic work

Upper Key Stage 2

  • Daily reading (20-30 minutes)
  • Weekly spelling and grammar
  • Weekly maths practice
  • Topic research projects

Reading

Reading is the most important homework activity. We ask that all children read at home every day. Please record this in your child's reading record, which should come to school daily.

  • Listen to your child read aloud
  • Ask questions about the story
  • Discuss new vocabulary
  • Read to your child, even when they can read independently

Online Learning Platforms

We use several online platforms to support home learning:

Times Tables Rock Stars
Spelling Shed
Oxford Owl
Purple Mash

Login details are provided by your child's class teacher.

Tips for Parents

  • Create a quiet, comfortable space for homework
  • Establish a regular homework routine
  • Be available to help, but encourage independence
  • Praise effort and celebrate achievements
  • Contact your child's teacher if there are any difficulties
