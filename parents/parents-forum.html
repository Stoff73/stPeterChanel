Parents Forum

Home/Parents/Parents Forum

The Parents Forum provides an opportunity for parents and carers to share their views and work together with the school.

What is the Parents Forum?

The Parents Forum is a group of parent representatives who meet regularly with the Headteacher and Senior Leadership Team. It provides a platform for two-way communication between parents and the school.

The forum is separate from the PTA - while the PTA focuses on fundraising and community events, the Parents Forum focuses on school policy, communication and the educational experience.

What We Discuss

  • School policies and procedures
  • Curriculum developments
  • Communication between home and school
  • School improvement priorities
  • Parent concerns and suggestions
  • Feedback on school initiatives

Get Involved

If you would like to become a class representative or share your views with the forum, please contact your class representative or the school office.

Your Voice Matters

We value parent feedback and use it to help shape our school. If you have suggestions or concerns, please speak to your class representative or contact the school directly.
