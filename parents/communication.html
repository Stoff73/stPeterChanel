Good communication between home and school is essential. We use a variety of methods to keep you informed about your child's education and school life.

Reading Records

Meeting with Teachers

We offer several opportunities to meet with your child's teacher:

  • Parents' Evenings: Held twice yearly in Autumn and Spring terms
  • Open Mornings: Opportunities to see your child's learning
  • End of Year Reports: Detailed written reports in the Summer term
  • Informal catch-ups: Teachers are available at the classroom door before and after school
  • Appointments: Contact the office to arrange a meeting if you have concerns

Reporting Absence

If your child is unwell and unable to attend school, please:

  • Contact the school office by 9:00am on the first day of absence
  • Call each day of absence unless a return date has been agreed
  • Provide a reason for the absence

You can report absence by phone or through the ParentMail app.

Making a Complaint

We hope you never need to make a complaint, but if you do have concerns:

  1. Speak to your child's class teacher in the first instance
  2. If unresolved, arrange to meet with a member of the Senior Leadership Team
  3. If still unresolved, contact the Headteacher
  4. Formal complaints can be made following our Complaints Policy
