School Uniform

Home/Parents/Uniform

We take pride in our school uniform. All children are expected to wear the correct uniform every day.

School Uniform

Everyday Uniform

  • Royal blue sweatshirt or cardigan with school logo
  • White polo shirt
  • Grey trousers, shorts, skirt or pinafore
  • Blue and white gingham dress (summer)
  • Black school shoes (no trainers)
  • Grey or white socks / grey tights

PE Kit

  • White t-shirt
  • Royal blue shorts
  • Black plimsolls or trainers
  • Navy or black tracksuit (outdoor PE / winter)
  • PE bag

Additional Information

  • Jewellery: No jewellery should be worn except small stud earrings and watches
  • Hair: Long hair must be tied back. Hair accessories should be blue, white or black
  • Nail varnish: Not permitted
  • Makeup: Not permitted
  • Labelling: Please label all items of clothing clearly with your child's name

Second-Hand Uniform

Our PTA runs a second-hand uniform service. Good quality, clean uniform can be purchased at a reduced cost. Please contact the school office for more information or check the PTA notice board for upcoming sales.

Lost Property

Lost property is kept in the school office. Please ensure all items are clearly labelled to help us return items to their owners. Unclaimed items are regularly donated to the second-hand uniform sale.
