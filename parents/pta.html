Our PTA brings together parents, staff and friends of the school to raise funds and build our school community.

About Our PTA

The PTA (Parent Teacher Association) is a volunteer-run organisation that works to support the school. We organise events, raise funds and help create a strong sense of community within our school family.

All parents and carers are automatically members of the PTA and are welcome to get involved, whether that's attending events, volunteering at fairs, or joining the committee.

What We Do

Events

We organise fun events throughout the year for children and families.

Fundraising

We raise money to support the school with resources and enrichment activities.

Community

We bring families together and help new parents feel welcome.

Annual Events

  • Welcome Back BBQ: September
  • Autumn Disco: October
  • Christmas Fair: December
  • Valentine's Disco: February
  • Easter Egg Hunt: March/April
  • Summer Fair: July
  • Plus: Second-hand uniform sales, cake sales, movie nights and more!

How Funds Are Used

Money raised by the PTA has helped fund:

  • Playground equipment and improvements
  • Library books and reading resources
  • ICT equipment
  • Educational enrichment activities
  • Subsidising school trips
  • End of year leavers' events

Get Involved

There are many ways to support the PTA:

  • Volunteer: Help at events - even an hour makes a difference!
  • Attend events: Come along to our fairs and discos
  • Join the committee: Help plan and organise events
  • Share skills: Do you have skills that could help? Let us know!
  • Donate: We welcome donations of prizes, raffle items and refreshments
