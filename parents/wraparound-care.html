Wraparound Care

Home/Parents/Wraparound Care

We offer wraparound care to support working families with before and after school childcare.

Breakfast Club

7:45am - 8:45am

Start the day right with our breakfast club. Children enjoy a healthy breakfast and supervised activities before school.

  • Choice of cereals, toast and fruit
  • Juice, milk or water
  • Indoor games and activities
  • Supervision by trained staff

Cost: £4.00 per session

After School Club

3:15pm - 6:00pm

A safe, fun environment for children after school with a range of activities and a light snack.

  • Outdoor and indoor play
  • Arts and crafts
  • Homework support
  • Healthy snack provided

Cost: £10.00 per session

How to Book

Places can be booked in advance through our online booking system or by contacting the school office. We offer:

  • Regular bookings: Book the same days each week for the term
  • Ad-hoc bookings: Book individual sessions as needed (subject to availability)
  • Emergency bookings: We try to accommodate last-minute requests where possible

Payment

Payment is made through our online payment system. Please ensure your account is kept in credit.

  • Payments can be made weekly, monthly or termly
  • Childcare vouchers and Tax-Free Childcare accepted
  • Some families may be eligible for help with childcare costs

Registration

All children attending wraparound care must be registered. Please complete the registration form and return it to the school office.
