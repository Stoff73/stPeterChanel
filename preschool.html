Pre-School

Home/ Pre-School

Our on-site Pre-School provides a nurturing, stimulating environment for children from age 2, giving them the best possible start to their educational journey.

Welcome to Our Pre-School

St Peter Chanel Pre-School is located on the same site as our primary school, allowing for a seamless transition when children move into Reception.

Our experienced and caring team create a warm, welcoming environment where children feel safe, secure and happy to learn through play.

What We Offer

Outdoor Learning

Access to our extensive school grounds for outdoor play

Our Approach

We follow the Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS) curriculum, focusing on the seven areas of learning:

Prime Areas

  • Communication and Language - developing speaking, listening and understanding skills
  • Physical Development - building fine and gross motor skills
  • Personal, Social and Emotional Development - building confidence, relationships and managing feelings

Specific Areas

  • Literacy - early reading and writing skills
  • Mathematics - number, shape, space and measure
  • Understanding the World - exploring people, places and technology
  • Expressive Arts and Design - creativity through art, music and imaginative play

A Typical Day

Each day includes a balance of:

  • Free play and child-initiated learning
  • Adult-led activities
  • Circle time and story time
  • Outdoor play
  • Snack time
  • Creative activities

Transition to Reception

Being on the same site as our primary school means that children benefit from a smooth transition into Reception. Pre-School children become familiar with the school environment and staff, making starting school an exciting rather than daunting experience.
