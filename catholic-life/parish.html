We work closely with our local parish and the Corpus Christi Partnership, sharing our faith and worshipping together.

Corpus Christi Parish

St Peter Chanel Catholic Primary School is part of the Corpus Christi Partnership, a group of Catholic parishes in the Bexley area. We have strong links with our parish church and the parish priest regularly visits our school.

Mass Times

Please check the parish website for any changes to Mass times.

Parish Links

Our school and parish work together in many ways:

  • Parish priest visits school for assemblies and Masses
  • School Masses celebrated in church
  • Sacramental preparation in partnership with the parish
  • Children participate in parish celebrations
  • Shared events and initiatives

Corpus Christi Partnership

The Corpus Christi Partnership brings together Catholic parishes across Bexley to work together, share resources and support each other in mission.

Visit the Parish

We encourage all families to be active members of their parish community. Regular attendance at Sunday Mass is an important part of Catholic life and supports children in living out their faith.
