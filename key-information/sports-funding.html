Primary School Sports Funding

Home/Key Information/Sports Funding

The PE and Sport Premium is additional funding provided to primary schools to improve the quality of PE and sport for all pupils.

What is the PE and Sport Premium?

The PE and Sport Premium is provided by the Department for Education to improve the provision of physical education and sport in primary schools. Schools must use the funding to make additional and sustainable improvements.

How We Use Our Funding

At St Peter Chanel, we use our PE and Sport Premium to:

  • Provide specialist PE coaching and CPD for teachers
  • Purchase new sports equipment
  • Enter inter-school sports competitions
  • Offer a wider range of sports and activities
  • Increase participation in extra-curricular sport
  • Support targeted groups to engage in physical activity

Impact

The funding has enabled us to:

  • Improve the quality of PE teaching across the school
  • Increase pupil participation in competitive sport
  • Offer a broader range of sports and activities
  • Develop staff confidence and competence in teaching PE
  • Improve facilities and resources for PE

Sports Provision

We offer a range of sports and physical activities including:

  • Football
  • Netball
  • Athletics
  • Gymnastics
  • Dance
  • Swimming (Year 4)
  • Multi-skills
  • Outdoor and adventurous activities
