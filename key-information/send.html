Special Educational Needs & Disabilities

Home/Key Information/SEND

At St Peter Chanel, we are committed to providing an inclusive education that meets the needs of all children, including those with Special Educational Needs and Disabilities (SEND).

Our Approach

We believe that every child is made in the image of God and deserves to reach their full potential. We work closely with families and external agencies to ensure that children with SEND receive the support they need to thrive.

Our SENCo

Our Special Educational Needs Coordinator (SENCo) leads on SEND provision across the school. If you have any concerns about your child's learning or development, please contact the school office to arrange a meeting.

How We Identify SEND

We identify children who may have SEND through:

  • Ongoing assessment and monitoring of progress
  • Teacher observations and concerns
  • Information from parents and carers
  • Liaison with previous settings
  • Screening and diagnostic assessments

Types of Support

Children with SEND may receive support through:

  • Quality First Teaching - Differentiated classroom teaching
  • SEN Support - Additional interventions and support
  • Education, Health and Care Plan (EHCP) - For children with more complex needs

The Four Areas of Need

SEND is broadly categorised into four areas:

  • Communication and Interaction - Including speech, language and autism
  • Cognition and Learning - Including specific learning difficulties
  • Social, Emotional and Mental Health
  • Sensory and/or Physical Needs

Working with Families

We believe in working in partnership with parents and carers. This includes:

  • Regular meetings to discuss progress and support
  • Involvement in setting and reviewing targets
  • Clear communication about interventions
  • Support with accessing external services

External Support

We work with a range of external agencies including:

  • Educational Psychologist
  • Speech and Language Therapy
  • Occupational Therapy
  • CAMHS (Child and Adolescent Mental Health Services)
  • Bexley SEND Services
