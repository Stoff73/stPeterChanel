How to Apply

Reception Admissions

Applications for Reception places are made through the London Borough of Bexley. The application window typically opens in September for the following academic year.

Please visit the Bexley Council website for more information about the application process and deadlines.

In-Year Admissions

If you are looking for a place outside the normal admissions round (for example, moving into the area), please contact the school office to enquire about availability.

Pre-School Admissions

Our on-site Pre-School accepts children from age 2. For Pre-School admissions, please contact us directly or visit www.spcpreschool.org.uk.

Admissions Policy

Our admissions arrangements are determined by the St Oscar Romero Catholic Academy Trust in consultation with the Diocese of Southwark.
